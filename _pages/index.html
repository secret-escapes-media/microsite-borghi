Discover the beautiful Borghi of Italy

Effortlessly romantic, these medieval towns and villages are typified by their irresistible rustic charms, exquisite local cuisine, and breathtaking beauty.

From the cool alpine reaches in the north down to the sun-drenched beaches of the southern tip, Italy is awash with a colourful collection of remarkable rural retreats. Borghi are part of the fabric of Italian life, where tradition in food and drink and in culture and architecture are upheld and venerated. Discover a culinary adventure, try irresistible wine, allow breathtaking landscapes to seduce and beguile, and lose yourself in the enchanting tapestry of Italy’s Borghi.
